WELCOME TO VIT AP

VIT-AP (Vellore Institute of Technology-Andhra Pradesh) is a prominent private university in India, established in 2017.
Located in Amaravati, Andhra Pradesh, it is an offshoot of the prestigious Vellore Institute of Technology (VIT) based in
Tamil Nadu. VIT-AP offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in engineering, technology,
management, and other fields. The university is known for its modern infrastructure, state-of-the-art laboratories, and a
strong emphasis on research and innovation. It aims to provide quality education and create skilled professionals to meet
the evolving demands of various industries. VIT-AP is recognized for its academic excellence and diverse student community.
